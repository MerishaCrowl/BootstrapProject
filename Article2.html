Domestic Violence Statistics in the US

3

The average number of women who will be murdered today in the US by their husband or boyfriend.(Source: U.S. Department of Justice, Bureau of Justice Statistics)

20

The average number of people who are physically abused by an intimate partner in the US every 60 SECONDS, that equates to more than 10 million women and men annually.(Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence)

1 in 4

The number of women who experience severe intimate partner physical violence, intimate partner contact sexual violence, and /or intimate partner stalking with impacts such as injury, fearfulness, post-traumatic stress disorder, use of victim services, contraction of sexually transmitted diseases, etc.(Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence)

1 in 4

The number of women who actually report the domestic violence they are experiencing.(Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence)

That means if you were standing in a room with 16 women, 4 of them have experienced domestic violence and only 1 reported it. Think about that next time you stand in line at the grocery store or sit in the arena at your next event.

19%

The percentage of domestic violence that involves a weapon.(Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence)

18-24

The most common age range a woman is abused by an intimate partner.(Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence) Remember this when you sisters and daughters begin dating and look for the signs.

1 in 3

The number of women who have experienced rape, physical violence, and /or stalking by an intimate partner in their lifetime.(Source: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention)

Domestic violence affects people of all races, ages, genders, and socioeconomic statuses. (Source: National Domestic Violence Hotline)

72%

The approximate number of murder-suicides involving an intimate partner.(Source: Violence Policy Center)

94%

The percentage of victims of murder-suicide that are women.(Source: Violence Policy Center)

Domestic Violence is the 3rd leading cause of homelessness among families.(Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence)

The presence of a gun in a domestic violence situation increases the risk of homicide by 500%. (Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence)

Children who witness domestic violence are at a greater risk for anxiety, depression, low self-esteem, and aggressive behavior. (Source: National Domestic Violence Hotline)

1,247

The number of women who were killed by an intimate partner in 2018.(Source: National Coalition Against Domestic Violence)

1,690

The number of women who were killed by an intimate partner in 2021. (Source: US Dept of Justice, Bureau of Justice Statistics)

The percentage of females murdered by an intimate partner was 5 TIMES higher than for males.(Source: US Dept of Justice, Bureau of Justice Statistics)
